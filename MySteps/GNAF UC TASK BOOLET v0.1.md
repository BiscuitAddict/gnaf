\# GNAF Universal Constitutional Task Booklet v0.1



\## Purpose



This booklet defines a common accountability framework that may be adapted to education, workforce development, nonprofits, research organizations, public programs, apprenticeships, contractors, and private organizations.



Every task category contains six constitutional questions.



No task is considered complete until all applicable questions are answered.



\---



\# STAGE 0 — FOUNDATIONAL GOVERNANCE



\## Task 1: Define Mission



Questions:



1\. What problem are we solving?

2\. Who benefits?

3\. What outcomes are expected?

4\. What risks exist?

5\. What evidence proves success?

6\. What activities are outside scope?
